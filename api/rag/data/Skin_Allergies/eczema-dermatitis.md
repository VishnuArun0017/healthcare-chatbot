## Overview

**Eczema (dermatitis)** is an inflammatory skin condition causing dry, itchy, red, or cracked skin.

## Common symptoms

- Dry, itchy patches  
- Redness or thickened skin  
- Cracking or oozing if infected  

## Red flags

- Sudden worsening  
- Signs of infection (oozing, yellow crusts, fever)  

## Self-care

- Moisturise often, especially after washing  
- Avoid scratching and harsh soaps  
- Use gentle, fragrance-free cleansers  

## Prevention

- Identify and avoid triggers (detergents, allergens)  
- Keep skin hydrated
