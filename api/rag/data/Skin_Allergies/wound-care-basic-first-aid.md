## Overview

**Wound care** helps prevent infection and supports healing after cuts or scrapes.

## First Aid Steps

1. Wash hands and clean the wound gently with water.  
2. Apply gentle pressure to stop bleeding.  
3. Cover with a sterile dressing or plaster.  
4. Change dressing daily or when wet.  

## Red flags

- Deep or dirty wound  
- Persistent bleeding  
- Redness, swelling, pus, or fever  

## Prevention

- Keep tetanus vaccinations up to date
