## Overview

A **burn** is skin damage from heat, chemicals, or electricity.  
Prompt first aid reduces complications.

## First Aid Steps

1. Cool burn under **running water for 20 minutes** (not ice).  
2. Remove jewellery or tight clothing near area.  
3. Cover with clean, non-fluffy material.  
4. Do **not** pop blisters or apply creams.  

## Red flags

- Burn larger than your palm  
- Face, hands, genitals, or joint involvement  
- Difficulty breathing or electrical burns  

## Prevention

- Keep hot liquids and chemicals out of reach of children  
- Use protective gear when cooking or handling heat
