## Overview

**Scabies** is a contagious skin infestation caused by tiny mites that burrow into the skin.

## Common symptoms

- Intense itching, worse at night  
- Fine lines or burrows on wrists, fingers, or waist  
- Rash in skin folds or between fingers  

## Red flags

- Itch persists despite hygiene  
- Family members also itching  

## Self-care

- Wash clothes, towels, and bedding in hot water  
- Avoid close skin contact until treated  

## Prevention

- Treat everyone in the household at the same time
