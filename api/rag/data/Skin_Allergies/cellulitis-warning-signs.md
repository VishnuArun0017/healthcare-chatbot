## Overview

**Cellulitis** is a bacterial skin infection affecting deeper layers of skin and tissue.

## Common symptoms

- Red, swollen, warm skin  
- Pain and tenderness  
- Fever or chills  

## Red flags

- Rapidly spreading redness  
- Fever, confusion, or feeling unwell  
- Red streaks from the area → medical emergency  

## Self-care

- Rest and elevate affected limb  
- Keep skin clean and moisturised  

## Prevention

- Treat cuts and insect bites early  
- Manage eczema or athlete’s foot
