## Overview / Definition

**Acne** is a common skin condition where hair follicles become blocked with oil and dead skin cells.  
It often appears on the face, chest, shoulders, or back.

## Common symptoms

- Blackheads, whiteheads, pimples  
- Oily skin or cysts  
- Possible pain or scarring  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Large, painful lumps (nodules)  
- Scarring or emotional distress  
- Sudden severe acne in adults  

## Self-care

- Gently wash face twice daily  
- Avoid picking or squeezing spots  
- Use non-comedogenic products  
- Eat balanced diet, stay hydrated  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Manage stress  
- Avoid heavy make-up or greasy creams
