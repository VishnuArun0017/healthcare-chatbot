## Overview

**Urticaria (hives)** causes raised, itchy red or white welts on the skin due to an allergic or immune reaction.

## Common symptoms

- Itchy bumps or welts  
- Flushing or swelling of skin  
- Comes and goes within hours or days  

## Red flags

- Swelling of lips, face, tongue, or throat  
- Difficulty breathing → call emergency services  

## Self-care

- Cool compresses  
- Avoid scratching and known triggers  
- Loose cotton clothing  

## Prevention

- Identify allergens (food, medication, insect bites)
