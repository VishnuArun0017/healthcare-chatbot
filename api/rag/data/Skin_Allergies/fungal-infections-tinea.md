## Overview

**Fungal infections (tinea)** affect skin, hair, or nails.  
They spread easily through skin contact or shared items.

## Common symptoms

- Circular red, itchy patches  
- Cracking between toes (athlete’s foot)  
- Scaly rash or ring-shaped lesion  

## Red flags

- Pain, swelling, or pus  
- Infection not improving with hygiene  

## Self-care

- Keep area clean and dry  
- Do not share towels or footwear  
- Wash clothes and bedding regularly  

## Prevention

- Use footwear in public showers  
- Maintain personal hygiene
