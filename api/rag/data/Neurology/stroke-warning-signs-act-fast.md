## Overview / Definition

A **stroke** occurs when blood supply to part of the brain is interrupted or reduced, leading to loss of function.

## Warning signs — “ACT FAST”

**F — Face:** drooping on one side  
**A — Arms:** weakness or numbness in one arm  
**S — Speech:** slurred or unclear speech  
**T — Time:** call emergency help immediately  

## Other possible symptoms

- Sudden vision loss  
- Severe headache  
- Loss of balance or coordination  

## Red flags

Every minute counts — **act FAST** if any symptom appears, even if temporary.

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Control blood pressure and diabetes  
- Stop smoking and exercise regularly  
- Balanced diet with less salt and processed food
