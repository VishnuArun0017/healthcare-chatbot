## Overview / Definition

A **tension-type headache** causes a **dull, tight, or pressure-like pain** around the head, neck, or scalp.  
It is often linked to **stress, tiredness, or poor posture**.

## Common symptoms

- Steady ache or pressure on both sides of the head  
- Tight neck or shoulder muscles  
- No nausea or vomiting  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek medical help if:
- Headache pattern changes suddenly  
- Occurs daily or disrupts life  
- Associated with vision, speech, or balance issues  

## Self-care

- Gentle neck and shoulder stretches  
- Relaxation or stress-management techniques  
- Maintain hydration and regular meals  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Maintain posture at work  
- Regular exercise and adequate rest  
- Avoid skipping meals and overuse of pain medicine
