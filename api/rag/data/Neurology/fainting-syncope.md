## Overview / Definition

**Fainting (syncope)** is a short loss of consciousness due to a temporary drop in blood flow to the brain.

## Common symptoms

- Feeling lightheaded or dizzy  
- Blurred vision or ringing in ears  
- Weakness or sweating before collapse  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Call emergency services if:
- Fainting during exercise or while lying down  
- Chest pain, palpitations, or shortness of breath  
- Prolonged loss of consciousness  

## Self-care / First aid

- Lay the person flat and raise legs  
- Loosen tight clothing  
- Once awake, help them sit up slowly  
- Seek medical review if recurrent  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Stay hydrated and avoid skipping meals  
- Rise slowly from sitting or lying positions
