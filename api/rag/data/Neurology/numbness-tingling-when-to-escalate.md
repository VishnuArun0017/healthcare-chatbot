## Overview / Definition

**Numbness or tingling** (pins and needles) happens when nerves are temporarily compressed or irritated.  
Occasional episodes are common and harmless, but sudden or persistent numbness can indicate a serious cause.

## Common symptoms

- Tingling or loss of feeling in hands, feet, or face  
- Temporary after sitting awkwardly  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek **urgent medical help** if:
- Sudden numbness/weakness on one side  
- Trouble speaking or vision changes  
- Numbness after head or neck injury  
- Persistent or spreading numbness  

## Self-care

- Move or stretch affected area if posture-related  
- Avoid tight clothing or prolonged pressure  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Stay active and maintain healthy posture  
- Manage diabetes or vitamin deficiencies per clinician advice
