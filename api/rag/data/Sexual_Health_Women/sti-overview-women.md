## Overview

**STIs** spread through sexual contact and may affect fertility or pregnancy if untreated.

## Common symptoms

- Abnormal discharge  
- Pain when urinating  
- Sores, warts, or itching  
- Bleeding after sex  

## Red flags

- Lower tummy pain or fever  
- Pain during sex  
- Missed periods or abnormal bleeding  

## Self-care

- Get tested if exposed or symptomatic  
- Avoid sexual activity until treated  
- Inform partners for testing  

## Prevention

- Use condoms correctly  
- Vaccinate for HPV and Hepatitis B
