## Overview

**Menopause** is when periods stop due to reduced estrogen levels, marking the end of fertility.

## Common symptoms

- Hot flushes and night sweats  
- Mood changes or poor sleep  
- Vaginal dryness, reduced libido  

## Self-care

- Light clothing, cool room at night  
- Regular exercise and balanced diet  
- Avoid smoking, limit alcohol  

## Red flags

- Bleeding after menopause  
- Severe mood or memory changes
