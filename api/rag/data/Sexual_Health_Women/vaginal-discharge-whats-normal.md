## Overview

**Vaginal discharge** is a normal body function that helps clean and protect the vagina.  
It changes with the menstrual cycle.

## Normal features

- Clear or white fluid  
- Mild odour  
- Increases mid-cycle or before period  

## Red flags

- Yellow, green, or thick discharge  
- Strong smell or itching  
- Pain, bleeding, or burning when urinating  

## Self-care

- Gentle washing with water only  
- Avoid douching or scented soaps  
- Wear breathable cotton underwear
