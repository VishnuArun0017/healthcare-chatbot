## Overview

**PCOS** is a hormonal condition where ovaries produce extra androgens (male hormones).  
It can cause irregular periods, weight gain, acne, or difficulty getting pregnant.

## Common symptoms

- Irregular or absent periods  
- Excess facial/body hair  
- Acne or oily skin  
- Weight gain or fertility problems  

## Red flags

- Sudden worsening of symptoms  
- Unexplained rapid weight gain  
- Signs of diabetes (thirst, fatigue)  

## Self-care

- Balanced diet and regular exercise  
- Weight management supports hormone balance  
- Stress control and adequate sleep  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Maintain healthy BMI  
- Monitor cycles and blood sugar
