## Overview

**UTI** affects the urinary tract; **vaginitis** affects the vagina.  
Both can cause discomfort but have different symptoms.

## UTI key features

- Burning or pain when peeing  
- Frequent urge to urinate  
- Cloudy or strong-smelling urine  

## Vaginitis key features

- Itching or soreness  
- Abnormal discharge or odour  
- Pain during sex  

## Self-care

- Stay hydrated  
- Practice good hygiene (front to back)  
- Avoid scented washes
