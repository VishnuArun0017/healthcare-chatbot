## Overview

**Emergency contraception** prevents pregnancy after unprotected sex or contraceptive failure.

## Options

- **Emergency pill:** effective within a few days  
- **Copper IUD:** most effective, can be used up to 5 days  

## Red flags

- Vomiting within 3 hours of taking a pill  
- No period within 3 weeks  

## Self-care

- Follow medical advice and instructions  
- Use protection moving forward
