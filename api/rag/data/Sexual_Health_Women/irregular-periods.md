## Overview

An **irregular period** means your cycle length or flow varies more than usual.  
It can result from stress, weight change, hormonal imbalance, or conditions like **PCOS**.

## Common causes

- Puberty, perimenopause  
- Stress or extreme exercise  
- Thyroid imbalance, PCOS, pregnancy  

## Red flags

- No period for >3 months  
- Very heavy or painful periods  
- Bleeding after sex or between periods  

## Self-care

- Keep a **period tracker**  
- Manage stress, eat balanced meals  
- Seek advice if irregularities persist
