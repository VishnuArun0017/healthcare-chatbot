## Overview

**Dyspareunia** means pain during or after sexual intercourse.  
It can have physical or emotional causes.

## Common causes

- Vaginal dryness  
- Infection or inflammation  
- Anxiety, trauma, or stress  

## Red flags

- Pain deep in pelvis  
- Bleeding or burning  
- Associated abnormal discharge  

## Self-care

- Adequate arousal and communication  
- Use water-based lubricant  
- Relaxation techniques  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Address underlying anxiety  
- Avoid scented products
