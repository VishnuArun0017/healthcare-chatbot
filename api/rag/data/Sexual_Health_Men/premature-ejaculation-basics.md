## Overview / Definition

**Premature ejaculation (PE)** occurs when a man ejaculates sooner than desired during sexual activity, often causing stress or frustration.

## Common causes

- Anxiety, stress, or relationship issues  
- Over-sensitivity  
- Hormonal or neurological factors (less common)  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Happens almost every time despite relaxation  
- Associated pain or erectile issues  
- Emotional distress or relationship strain  

## Evidence-backed self-care

- Relaxation and pause–squeeze methods  
- Focus on non-penetrative intimacy  
- Avoid rushing or performance pressure  
- Discuss openly with partner  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Manage stress  
- Avoid alcohol or drug use before intercourse  
- Pelvic floor exercises may help
