## Overview / Definition

**Erectile difficulty** means trouble achieving or maintaining an erection firm enough for sexual activity.  
It’s common and often linked to **stress, tiredness, or health issues**.

## Common causes

- Stress, anxiety, depression  
- Alcohol or smoking  
- Diabetes, high blood pressure, heart disease  
- Certain medications  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek medical care if:
- Difficulty lasts more than a few weeks  
- Pain, curvature, or deformity of penis  
- Morning erections absent or reduced suddenly  
- Other symptoms like chest pain or fatigue  

## Self-care

- Regular exercise and healthy diet  
- Stop smoking and reduce alcohol  
- Manage stress and sleep well  
- Avoid recreational drugs  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Manage chronic conditions (diabetes, hypertension)  
- Maintain open communication with partner
