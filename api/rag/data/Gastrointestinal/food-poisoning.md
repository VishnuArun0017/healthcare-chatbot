## Overview

Food poisoning follows eating contaminated food or water.

## Symptoms

- Nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea, cramps, fever  

## Red flags

- Bloody stool, high fever, dehydration  

## Self-care

- Sip fluids, rest, avoid preparing food for others  

## Prevention

- Cook food thoroughly, wash hands, separate raw foods
