## Overview / Definition

Pain in the upper abdomen may come from indigestion, reflux, or gallbladder/liver.  
Common and often linked to meals.

## Common symptoms

- Burning, bloating, nausea  
- Worse after eating or lying down  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Sudden severe pain, black stools, vomiting blood  
- Unexplained weight loss, chest pain  

## Self-care

- Eat smaller meals, avoid late-night eating  
- Reduce alcohol and rich foods  
- Raise head of bed  

## Prevention

- Regular meals, healthy weight, stop smoking
