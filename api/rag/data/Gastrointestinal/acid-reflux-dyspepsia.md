## Overview

Reflux is stomach acid rising into the oesophagus, causing heartburn or sour taste.

## Symptoms

- Burning chest pain, bloating, regurgitation  

## Red flags

- Difficulty swallowing, vomiting blood, weight loss  

## Self-care

- Small meals, avoid lying flat  
- Reduce triggers like caffeine, alcohol, spicy food  

## Prevention

- Stop smoking, healthy weight
