## Overview

IBS causes chronic abdominal pain, bloating, and altered bowel habits without structural disease.

## Symptoms

- Cramps, bloating, diarrhoea or constipation, mucus in stool  

## Red flags

- Blood in stool, weight loss, fever, persistent change after age 55  

## Self-care

- Regular meals, fluids, manage stress  
- Limit caffeine, fizzy drinks, and alcohol
