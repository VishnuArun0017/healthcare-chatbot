## Overview

Jaundice is yellowing of skin or eyes due to excess bilirubin in blood.

## Symptoms

- Yellow skin/eyes, dark urine, pale stool, fatigue  

## Red flags

- New jaundice with pain, confusion, bleeding, fever  

## Self-care

- None — needs urgent assessment  

## Prevention

- Avoid alcohol, follow hepatitis vaccination
