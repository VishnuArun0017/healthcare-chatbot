## Overview

Lower tummy pain is common and may relate to bowel, urinary, or reproductive causes.

## Symptoms

- Cramping, bloating, or tenderness  

## Red flags

- Fever, vomiting, rectal bleeding  
- Pain not improving or recurring  

## Self-care

- Rest, fluids, light meals  
- Note bowel and urinary changes
