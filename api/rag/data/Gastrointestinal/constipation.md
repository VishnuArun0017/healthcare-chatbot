## Overview

Constipation means fewer or difficult bowel movements.

## Symptoms

- Hard stools, bloating, straining  

## Red flags

- Blood in stool, weight loss, persistent pain  

## Self-care

- Drink fluids  
- Increase fibre gradually  
- Be active and follow toilet urge  

## Prevention

- Regular meals, routine toilet timing
