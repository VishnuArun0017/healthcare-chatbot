## Overview / Definition

Feeling sick (nausea) or vomiting often follows infection or food issues.  
Most cases improve within 1–2 days.

## Common symptoms

- Nausea, stomach pain, tiredness  
- Loss of appetite  
- Risk of dehydration  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek care if:
- Can’t keep fluids down 24+ hours  
- Vomit blood or black material  
- High fever, severe pain, confusion  

## Evidence-backed self-care

- Sip small amounts of fluid often  
- Rest and avoid strong smells  
- Eat light foods when ready  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Hand hygiene and safe food handling  

## Special considerations

Pregnancy, medicine side effects, or alcohol may worsen nausea — consult clinician.
