## Overview / Definition

Knee pain may follow **injury, overuse, or sudden movement**.  
Soft tissue or ligament sprains are common causes.

## Common symptoms

- Pain, swelling, stiffness  
- Clicking or locking sensation  
- Difficulty walking or bending knee  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Sudden swelling after injury  
- Inability to straighten or bear weight  
- Fever or redness (possible infection)  

## Self-care

- **Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation (RICE)**  
- Avoid heavy use; gentle movement when able  
- Pain usually improves within days  

## Prevention

- Warm-up before exercise  
- Strengthen leg muscles
