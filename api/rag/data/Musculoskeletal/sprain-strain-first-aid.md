## Overview / Definition

A sprain is a **ligament injury**, while a strain affects **muscles or tendons**.  
Both often occur after a fall, twist, or overuse.

## Common symptoms

- Pain, swelling, bruising, or stiffness  
- Limited movement near affected joint  

## First Aid / Self-care

Follow **RICE**:
- **Rest:** limit movement for 24–48 hours  
- **Ice:** apply cold pack up to 20 minutes at a time  
- **Compression:** use bandage lightly  
- **Elevation:** raise limb above heart level  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Severe swelling, numbness, or inability to bear weight  
- Visible deformity or bone out of place  

## Prevention

- Warm-up before activity  
- Wear supportive footwear
