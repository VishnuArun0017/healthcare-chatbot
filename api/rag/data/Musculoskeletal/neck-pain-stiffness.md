## Overview / Definition

Neck pain involves **discomfort or stiffness in the muscles, joints, or nerves of the neck**.  
It is common after poor posture, strain, or sleeping awkwardly.

## Common symptoms / warning signs

- Pain or tightness in neck or shoulders  
- Reduced movement  
- Headache or muscle tenderness  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Pain after trauma (e.g., accident)  
- Weakness, tingling, or numbness in arms  
- Fever, severe stiffness, or difficulty moving head  

## Self-care

- Gentle **neck stretches** and posture correction  
- **Heat packs** or warm showers ease stiffness  
- Avoid sudden twisting or heavy lifting  

## Prevention

- Take regular breaks from screens  
- Adjust monitor to eye level  
- Use supportive pillow
