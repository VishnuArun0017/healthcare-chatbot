## Overview / Definition

Osteoarthritis (OA) is the most common arthritis type — caused by **joint cartilage wear and tear**.  
Usually affects knees, hips, hands, or spine.

## Common symptoms

- Joint pain, stiffness, or cracking sounds  
- Worse after activity, eased by rest  
- Swelling or reduced flexibility  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Rapid worsening pain  
- Joint locks or gives way  
- Swelling with redness or warmth  

## Self-care

- Stay active with **low-impact exercise** (walking, swimming)  
- Maintain healthy weight  
- Heat or cold packs can relieve discomfort  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Avoid prolonged inactivity  
- Strengthen supporting muscles  

## Special considerations

- Long-term OA may require physiotherapy or joint replacement evaluation
