## Overview / Definition

Low back pain refers to **pain or stiffness in the lower spine area**.  
Most cases are **muscle-related** and improve within a few weeks.

## Common symptoms / warning signs

- Dull ache or sharp pain in lower back  
- Stiffness after sitting or sleeping  
- Pain radiating to buttocks or legs (sciatica)  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek **urgent medical advice** if:
- Numbness in legs, loss of bladder/bowel control  
- Fever or unexplained weight loss  
- History of trauma or cancer  
- Pain that worsens or lasts >6 weeks  

## Evidence-backed self-care

- Stay active; gentle movement supports recovery  
- Apply **heat** or **cold packs** for short-term relief  
- Use good posture and avoid prolonged bed rest  
- Gradual return to normal activities  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Regular exercise (core strengthening, walking)  
- Ergonomic sitting and lifting techniques  

## Special considerations

- Persistent pain needs physiotherapy or imaging guidance.
