## Overview / Definition

Gout is a **form of arthritis** caused by **uric acid crystal buildup** in joints.  
It usually affects the big toe, ankles, or knees.

## Common symptoms

- Sudden, severe joint pain (often at night)  
- Red, hot, swollen joint  
- Limited motion  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Severe or first-time attack  
- Fever or signs of infection  
- Pain not improving after a few days  

## Self-care

- Rest the affected joint  
- Apply cold packs  
- Drink water and avoid alcohol  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Maintain healthy weight  
- Avoid excess red meat and sugary drinks  
- Stay hydrated
