## Overview / Definition

Burning urination (dysuria) means **pain or discomfort while passing urine**.  
Common cause is infection or irritation of the urinary tract.

## Common symptoms

- Pain or burning when urinating  
- Needing to pee frequently or urgently  
- Cloudy urine  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek care if:
- Fever, flank pain, blood in urine  
- Unable to pass urine  
- Persistent pain >48 hours  

## Self-care

- Hydrate well  
- Avoid caffeine and alcohol temporarily  
- Do not hold urine for long periods  

## Prevention

- Practice genital hygiene  
- Pee after sexual activity  
- Avoid scented soaps in genital area
