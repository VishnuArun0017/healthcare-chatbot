## Overview / Definition

Needing to pee often with increased thirst may signal conditions such as **diabetes** or **urinary infections**.  

## Common symptoms

- Passing urine more often than usual  
- Feeling very thirsty  
- Fatigue, blurred vision (if related to high blood sugar)  

## Red flags & when to escalate

Seek medical help if:
- Thirst and urination persist for days  
- Unexplained weight loss  
- Nighttime urination disrupting sleep  

## Self-care

- Stay hydrated with water  
- Avoid sugary drinks  
- Record frequency and discuss with clinician  

## Prevention

- Maintain balanced diet and regular check-ups
