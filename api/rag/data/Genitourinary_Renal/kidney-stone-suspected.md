## Overview / Definition

Kidney stones are **hard mineral deposits** that form in the urinary tract and can block urine flow.  

## Common symptoms

- Sharp pain in back, side, or lower abdomen  
- Pain spreading to groin  
- Blood in urine  
- Nausea or vomiting  

## Red flags & when to escalate

- Severe pain that comes in waves  
- Fever or chills  
- Unable to pass urine  
- Persistent vomiting  

## Self-care

- Keep hydrated while waiting for medical care  
- Use heat pack for pain (if advised by clinician)  

## Prevention / lifestyle

- Drink enough water daily  
- Limit excess salt and high-oxalate foods
